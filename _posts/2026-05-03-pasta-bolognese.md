Classic pasta sauce with beef, tomatoes and carrots.

# Instructions

- Grate the carrots.
- Mince the garlic cloves and the onion. Brown them in olive oil.
- Add the ground beef and stir until brown.
- Add the crushed tomatoes and the grated carrots.
- Season with salt, Italian herbs and a bit of cayenne pepper.
- Let simmer for **30 minutes**.

Serve with pasta.
