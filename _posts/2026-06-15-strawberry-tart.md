Classic French strawberry tart on a sweet crust with vanilla pastry cream.

# Instructions

- Make a [sand crust]({{ "" | relative_url }}{% post_url 2026-06-15-sand-crust %}) and blind bake it.
- Prepare the [crème pâtissière]({{ "" | relative_url }}{% post_url 2026-06-15-creme-patissiere %}) and let it cool.
- Wash the strawberries and slice them lengthwise.
- Spread the cooled crème pâtissière over the cooled crust.
- Arrange the strawberries on top. Serve chilled.
