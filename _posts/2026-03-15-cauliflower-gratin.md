Cauliflower gratin with curry bechamel sauce

# Instructions

- Clean cauliflower and cut the heads. Steam it for 20 minutes.
- While the cauliflower steam, preheat the oven to **180°C** (356°F).
- Make a [Béchamel sauce]({{ "" | relative_url }}{% post_url 2026-03-15-bechamel %}), season it with curry and paprika.
- Put the cauliflower in a gratin dish, pour the Béchamel on top.
- Add some grated Emmental (can be substituted with sharp cheddar).
- Put in the oven for 30 minutes. Broil for the last 5 minutes if it isn't golden enough.
