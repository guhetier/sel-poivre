A Jamaican-inspired pork sauté with caramelised tropical fruits, served with couscous.

# Instructions

## Pork sauté
- Cut the pork shoulder into small chunks and brown in **30g** of butter for **10 minutes**.
- Soften the sliced onions in a casserole with **30g** of butter. Add the minced garlic, nutmeg and chili. Season with salt and pepper.
- Deglaze with the white wine.
- Cover and cook on medium heat for **45 minutes**, stirring occasionally.
- Add the rum and lemon slices. Continue cooking uncovered for **30 minutes**.

## Caramelized fuits
- Peel and slice the bananas and pineapple.
- Sauté in a pan with **25g** of butter.
- Sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.

## Couscous
- Pour **30cL** of boiling salted water over the couscous.
- Add the remaining **40g** of butter.
- Wait a few minutes until fully absorbed.

Serve the couscous in a deep dish topped with the caramelised fruits. Serve the pork hot on the side.
