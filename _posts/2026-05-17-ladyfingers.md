      - Baking powder, ¼ tsp (3g)

Light, crisp sponge biscuits used for tiramisu and trifles.
Yields ~24 biscuits.

# Instructions

- Preheat oven to **205°C** (400°F). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper. Fit a piping bag with a plain **1cm** round tip.
- Separate the eggs.
- Beat egg whites to soft peaks. Add **25g** of the sugar and continue beating until stiff and glossy.
- In a separate bowl, beat egg yolks with the remaining **40g** sugar until thick and pale, about **3 min**.
- Fold half of the whites into the yolks.
- Sift flour and baking powder over the mixture and fold gently until nearly combined. Fold in the remaining whites just until incorporated.
- Transfer to the piping bag. Pipe **7cm** fingers, spaced **2-3cm** apart, on the baking sheets.
- Bake **8 min**, until firm and just golden.
- Cool completely on the baking sheets before using or storing.
