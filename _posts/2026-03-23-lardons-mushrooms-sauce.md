      - Sliced mushrooms, ~1 can
      - White cooking wine, 10cL
      - Olive oil, 1 tbsp
      - Salt, pepper
---

A creamy sauce, goes well with pasta, rice, couscous, vegetables...

# Instructions

- Mince the onion and the lardons.
- Sauté the onion in olive oil in a frying pan.
- Add the lardons and white wine. Sauté for **5 minutes**.
- Add the mushrooms and cream. Season.
- Simmer over low heat for **5 minutes**.

# Notes

- The lardons can be replaced with ham
