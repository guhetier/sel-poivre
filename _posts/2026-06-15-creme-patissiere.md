Vanilla pastry cream for filling tarts, choux, and éclairs.

# Instructions

- Heat the milk in a saucepan until just boiling.
- Whisk the eggs with the sugar until pale and the sugar is dissolved.
- Add the flour and whisk until smooth and lump-free.
- Pour half the hot milk over the egg mixture, whisking gently then more vigorously to loosen the cream.
- Return the mixture to the saucepan with the remaining milk.
- Cook over low heat, whisking constantly, until the cream thickens and the first bubbles appear. It should stay supple.
- Remove from heat, stir in the vanilla, and keep whisking briefly to cool it down.
- Dab the surface with the diced butter to prevent a skin from forming and to keep the cream glossy.

## Notes

Use **30g** or **50g** of flour for a more liquid or solid crème pat.
