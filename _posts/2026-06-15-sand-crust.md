      - Water, 30mL
      - Salt, a pinch

Sweet, crumbly crust used for fruit tarts and pastries.

# Instructions

- Cut the cold butter in small cubes.
- Rub the butter into the flour until fully absorbed and the mixture looks sandy.
- Add the icing sugar and mix in.
- Add the egg yolk, cold water, and salt. Mix only until the dough comes together; overworking makes it elastic.
- Wrap in plastic film and chill for **30 min**.
- Roll on a lightly floured surface, using as little flour as possible.

## Blind baking

For no-bake or wet fillings:
- Blind-bake at **180°C** (356°F) for **15 min** with weights.
- (optional) Seal by brushing with an egg diluted in water.
- Bake **5 min** more without weights.
