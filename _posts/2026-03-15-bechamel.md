Essential sauce for French cooking. Goes with gratins, lasagna, and many more.

# Instructions

- Melt the butter in a pot at medium heat.
- Add the flour and whisk to make a roux.
- Pour the milk while whisking.
- Set the heat to high, keep whisking until the sauce thickens.
- Remove from the heat and add salt, pepper and optional seasonings.
