No-bake Italian dessert with mascarpone and coffee.

# Instructions

See [ladyfingers]({{ "" | relative_url }}{% post_url 2026-05-17-ladyfingers %}) for home-made ladyfingers.

## Mascarpone cream

- Whip the egg yolks and **50g** of the sugar until thick and pale.
- Whip the cream and the remaining **50g** of sugar to soft peaks.
- Add the mascarpone and continue whipping until spreadable.
- Fold the yolks into the mascarpone mixture.

## Assembly

- Mix the espresso and the liqueur in a wide bowl.
- Dip ladyfingers in the coffee mixture for a few seconds, then arrange in a layer in a baking dish.
- Spread half of the mascarpone mixture on top.
- Add another layer of soaked ladyfingers, then the remainder of the mascarpone.
- Dust the top with cocoa powder.
- Chill for at least **4 hours**, preferably overnight.

# Notes

- Strong coffee from a moka pot can replace the espresso.
- Can be frozen once assembled. Dust with cocoa after thawing.
