Vegetarian lasagna layered with ricotta-spinach filling and tomato sauce.

# Instructions

Preheat to **190°C** (375°F).

## Tomato sauce

- Soften the onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat for **5 min**.
- Add the tomatoes and oregano. Bring to a boil and simmer **15 min**. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.

## Filling

- Defrost the spinach in the microwave. Drain in a strainer, pressing out excess liquid.
- In a bowl, combine the spinach with the ricotta, parmesan, cream, and nutmeg. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.

## Assembly

- Pre-cook the lasagna sheets if needed.
- Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce. Cover with a layer of pasta, then spread half the ricotta-spinach filling.
- Continue: tomato sauce, pasta, filling, pasta, tomato sauce.
- Top with grated mozzarella.
- Bake for **35 min**. Finish under the broiler. Rest **5 min** before serving.

# Notes

- Can be frozen after assembly, add **10 min** when baking from frozen.
