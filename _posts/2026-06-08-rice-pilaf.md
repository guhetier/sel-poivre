      - Water, ~470mL
      - Onion, 1
      - Olive oil, 2 tsp

Rice browned in oil with onion, then simmered in stock.

# Instructions

- Dissolve the stock cube in the water (twice the volume of rice).
- Chop the onion and brown in olive oil, in a large skillet.
- Add the rice and brown for **5 min** over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally.
- Season with salt and pepper.
- Pour in the stock, bring to a simmer, cover, reduce the heat, and cook **~18 min**. Do not uncover.
- Off the heat, let rest covered for **5 min**.
